Our Story

From a small clinic in Ranchi to a global beacon of hope — discover how Curesia is transforming mental health care with compassion and innovation.

2027

No Mental Health Stigma

By 2027, Curesia envisions a world where mental health care is universally accessible and stigma-free. We're building a global network of 50+ mental health centers and pioneering virtual reality therapy.

2025

The Future

As we step into 2025, Curesia is pioneering the next generation of mental health care. We're launching AI-powered therapy tools, and introducing innovative programs for workplace mental wellness.

2024

A Legacy of Healing

Today, Curesia has touched over 10,000 lives across 15+ countries. From a small clinic to a mental health care provider with passion for compassion, our mission remains the same: "Mental health is not a privilege, it's a right—and healing is possible for everyone."

2020

Going Global

When the world needed mental health support more than ever, Curesia embraced technology. We launched our online therapy platform, making professional mental health care accessible to anyone, anywhere in the world.

2018

Expanding Horizons

Recognizing the need for comprehensive mental health care, we expanded our services to include adult psychiatry, family therapy, and emergency crisis intervention. Our team grew from 1 to 8 dedicated professionals.

2015

First Breakthrough

After helping over 500 families rebuild their lives, Curesia became the trusted name for child psychology in Ranchi. Our early intervention approach proved that timely support could prevent lifelong struggles.

2014

The Seed of Compassion

Dr. Omi Singh, a young psychiatrist with a vision, noticed the growing mental health crisis among children and adolescents in Ranchi. With just a small clinic and an unwavering belief that "every child deserves to be heard," Curesia was born.
